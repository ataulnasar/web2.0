Biography

Since the early 1980's did Ikramini believe in the power of the "story" , because what is life but the stories we weave every day! Our lives are the daily stories we are telling and living in colors, forms and music. In Ikramini we love to tell our stories with style! We surely incorporate all the elements to represent them like a feast to your senses. We love to engage our audience and their senses in the storytelling process staying true to our purpose and that is giving our audience the best experience and products. Ikramini is considered one of the pioneers to represent the middle eastern heritage and our magical stories to the eastern and European market. Performing well known stories as well as our own productions using European crews in a blended theatrical and musical fashion shows. Since our very beginnings we were sure that our products are of the best quality and value! All our products are handmade with lots of passion and creativity and the best raw materials we can find. We hope you enjoy our work as we enjoy making you the most unique pieces of artwork that is one of their kinds. Owning one of our pieces is like owning an original painiting that has no replica. Our motto is always to deliver you the best. We believe that you are part of the designing process so please do follow us and interact with us through our social medias and be part of the magic making!
